faded
Discover new music from independent artists.

Faded takes the struggle out of discovery. Let it know your favourite genres, and it will start an infinite-stream of music you'll like from Bandcamp.

Download here
How do I use it?

Enter a genre into the search bar, and faded will suggest tags you might like. Clicking on a tag will add it to your playlist, and immediately begin streaming music.

Like what you hear? Click on the album name and it'll take you directly to the Bandcamp page where you can buy/download the album, find more of their work, and browse merch.
